Our Future

ISRO’s vision is to harness space technology for national development and to benefit humanity.

Founded

ISRO was founded in 1969 and is headquartered in Bengaluru, India.

NavIC

ISRO has also played a significant role in developing India’s indigenous satellite navigation system, NavIC.

First Satellite

ISRO built India’s first satellite, Aryabhata, launched into space by the Soviet Union in 1975.

On Mars

ISRO! Did this.

The Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM), unofficially termed as Mangalyaan, (from Sanskrit: Mangala, "Mars" and yāna, "craft, vehicle") was a space probe orbiting Mars since 24 September 2014.
It was launched on 5 November 2013 by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).
